About Us

Providing global customers with extreme convenience to access products from all over the world

Cross Cart is operated by want.jp Inc., a company dedicated to driving the next standard for global commerce. We provide international shopping services that connect customers worldwide to products from around the globe.

「want」ways

  • Ownership

    At want, every member is a stakeholder in the mission. Each of us pursues long-term value while focusing on short-term results. Everything that happens at want is matter of your own interest. While fulfilling your own role, you act with a broader perspective to benefit the entire company.

  • Openness & Commitment

    We believe in the importance of open communication no matter our job titles. If we disagree, we must respectfully discuss the issue until we reach a mutual understanding. We should avoid settling for light compromises and superficial harmony as it will lead us away from our mission. Once we reach a decision we must fully commit ourselves, regardless of our previously held viewpoints.

  • Data-Driven Decisions

    Facts and data outweigh subjective opinions. We define our true issues based on facts and data, accordingly calculate the risks, and make decisions. To ensure swift and accurate decision-making, we must regularly review and analyze the facts and data.

  • Tackle

    Speed and risk-taking are essential for achieving our mission. We must not delay decisions and actions by spending too much time on excessive research and deliberation. Since many decisions are reversible, it is worthwhile to actively take calculated risks, as this will allow us to move closer in achieving our mission.

  • Scalable System & Team

    We do not like to have our work and processes individually customized. It is necessary to standardize our operations and systems by working backwards from highly automated approaches. While it is common for work to be disrupted without certain individuals, we must put high priority on creating systems that can independently operate even without their presence.

  • Transparency

    It is crucial for all information to be open and accessible. When all members are open about their decisions and their decision-making process, it further strengthens the organization and connects each individual's actions with the mission. As every member proactively shares and obtains information, we must always consider how our own and team's actions align with the overall mission of the company.

About Cross Cart

Cross Cart is our international shopping service that embodies these values and mission. We specialize in helping customers purchase products from Japanese retailers and ship them worldwide, providing the convenience and accessibility that global commerce should offer.

Why Choose Cross Cart

With our deep knowledge of the Japanese market and expertise in international logistics, we provide a reliable and seamless shopping experience. Our team ensures that you can access Japan's unique products with confidence, backed by professional service and support.
