✅ Available Foods (Room Temperature)

  • Snacks & Sweets: Chocolates (room temp), cookies, gummies, candies, potato chips, nuts, dried fruits, etc.
  • Seasonings & Ingredients: Soy sauce, cooking sake, dressings, salt, pepper, olive oil, curry roux, etc.
  • Dried & Preserved Foods: Seaweed, dried noodles, rice, instant noodles, retort pouches, canned foods, etc.

❌ Prohibited Foods

Alcoholic BeveragesPlum wine, fruit wine, cooking sake with alcohol, mirin, etc.
Refrigerated / Frozen FoodsRaw meat, raw fish, fresh milk, frozen foods, ice cream, fresh vegetables, etc.
Raw EggsChicken eggs, quail eggs, etc.
Refrigerated SweetsFresh chocolates, fresh cakes, kimchi, natto (if refrigeration is required), etc.

*Items that only state "Refrigerate after opening" are acceptable. Items that explicitly require refrigeration or freezing for storage are prohibited.

All beverages containing alcohol are prohibited.

Beer & SimilarBeer, craft beer, etc.
WineRed wine, white wine, sparkling wine, champagne, etc.
Japanese Sake & ShochuSake, Junmai, Shochu (sweet potato, barley, rice), etc.
Spirits & LiqueursWhisky, gin, vodka, rum, plum wine, cocktails, etc.

Under CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species), international trade in endangered wild animals, plants, and their products is regulated.

❌ Prohibited Items

  • Live Animals & Plants: Live animals (reptiles, birds, amphibians, etc.), live plants (cacti, orchids, etc.)
  • Ivory & Elephant Products: Ivory seals, decorations, and carvings; elephant leather products
  • Reptile Leather & Products: Bags, wallets, belts, shoes made from crocodile, snake, lizard, or other reptile skins
  • Other Endangered Wildlife Products: Tiger/leopard fur and bones, rhino horn products, tortoiseshell (hawksbill turtle) products
  • Rare Plant Products: Products derived from regulated cacti, orchids, etc.

*Even if labeled "synthetic leather" or "faux leather," items will be prohibited if found to contain CITES-regulated materials.
